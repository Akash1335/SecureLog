How SecureLog Works 3-Factor Auth
1
Register

Create an account with your username, email and password. Your password is never stored directly — only a secure hash.

2
Challenge Handshake

On login, the server sends a one-time nonce. Your browser combines it with your password to produce a unique response key — so your password never travels over the network.

3
Email OTP

A 6-digit code is sent to your registered email as a second factor. Both factors must pass to grant access.

4
Session JWT

On success, a signed token is issued for your session. All steps are logged live in this panel.
